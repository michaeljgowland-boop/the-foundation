Article body content goes here as an HTML fragment — no <html>, <head>, or <body> tags. Just the content itself, using standard HTML elements: paragraphs, headings (h2, h3), blockquotes, and so on.

Section heading

The article template will apply the Foundation's typographic styles automatically. A h2 will render in Cormorant Garamond at the appropriate size; an h3 will render in small-caps Cinzel as a section label.

This approach keeps your content files clean and portable — they are HTML fragments that could be rendered in any context, and all the presentation logic lives in the CSS.
